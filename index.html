Hi there! I'm Ignacio, a designveloper and weblover with lots of common sense, working in ThoughtWorks.

I really love ThoughtWorks! It means working with some of the most awesome programmers I've ever met, doing stuff like real TDD/BDD, pair programming, agile development, one-click deployment and continuous delivery...

During more than five years I've been working with Drupal and leading the frontend team in more than 30 projects, so dealing with responsive web design, grids, SEO, CSS frameworks and HTML5/CSS3 is a real pleasure.

Also, my creative side is full of love for grahic design, creativity, usability and IU/UX, so there is plenty of stuff around Behance, flickr, 500px or Instagram, even some videos in vimeo or music in soundcloud.

Nowadays...

  • Reading The Design of Everyday Things — examples are quite outdated (the book is 25 years old!), and even the author is thinking about different approaches but the book is a completely must-to-read.
